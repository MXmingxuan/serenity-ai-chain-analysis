## Effect On Next Step

Search results support treating onsite/behind-the-meter gas generation as a real but conditional data-center demand pull. The strongest direct order/project evidence is currently outside China, especially Wartsila and Mitsubishi Power. China-listed candidates should be graded more conservatively unless they show direct data-center order or revenue exposure.

Component-level search changes the next step: the candidate universe should now include hot gas path parts, superalloy materials, precision casting, coating/process and aftermarket-service names. These names should not be upgraded unless component exposure is supported by orders, OEM qualification, revenue mix, or capacity/lead-time evidence.
